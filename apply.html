Application Form for Builder Rank

Thank you for your interest in joining our community! To apply for Builder rank on City Loopers MC Server, please fill out the form below. Please note that all applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Have built and contributed to the server in a meaningful way, either through the creation of new structures or the improvement of existing ones.
  • OR Have a minimum of 1 year of active participation in the server.
  • AND Have a positive and respectful attitude towards other players, and be willing to contribute to the community in a meaningful way.
  • AND Have demonstrated a willingness to follow and adhere to the server's rules and regulations.

Please note that this is a subjective process and we reserve the right to decline any application for any reason.
